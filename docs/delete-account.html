Delete Your Ibtikar Account

This page explains how to delete your Ibtikar account and all associated data. This process is permanent and cannot be undone.

⚠️ Important Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and irreversible. All your data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. Please read this page carefully before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Ibtikar app on your Android or iOS device
  2. Navigate to your profile or main screen
  3. Find the "Logout" button (typically located in the top-right corner with a logout icon)
  4. Tap "Logout" - This will log you out and clear your local app data
  5. Contact us to complete deletion (see below) - To permanently delete your account data from our servers, please email us at ibtikarpal@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include your Twitter/X username

Note: Currently, account deletion from our servers requires manual processing. We are working on implementing an automated account deletion feature within the app. In the meantime, please contact us via email to request account deletion.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Account Information:
    • Your Ibtikar user account and profile
    • Your Twitter/X user ID and username
    • Your display name and profile image URL
    • Your email address (if stored)
  • Authentication Data:
    • Your Twitter/X OAuth access tokens (encrypted)
    • Your Twitter/X OAuth refresh tokens (encrypted)
    • All OAuth connection information
  • Content Data:
    • All analyzed posts and tweets from your feed
    • Post content, text, and metadata
    • Post IDs, author IDs, and timestamps
    • Language classifications
  • Analysis Results:
    • All AI classification results (harmful/safe/unknown labels)
    • Confidence scores for each analysis
    • Analysis timestamps
    • Aggregate statistics and summaries
  • App Data:
    • Your activation status and preferences
    • App settings and configurations
    • Error logs associated with your account

Data Retention Period

Deletion Timeline:

  • Immediate: Your account data is deleted immediately from our active databases upon confirmation
  • Within 30 Days: All backup copies are permanently deleted
  • No Retention: We do not retain any of your data after deletion
  • No Recovery: Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered or restored

What Happens After Deletion

  • App Access: You will no longer be able to log in to the Ibtikar app with your account
  • Data Removal: All your analyzed posts, classifications, and data will be permanently removed
  • Twitter/X Account: Your Twitter/X account will remain unchanged - we only remove our access to it
  • OAuth Connection: The connection between Ibtikar and your Twitter/X account will be severed
  • New Account: You can create a new Ibtikar account at any time, but your previous data will not be restored
  • Historical Data: All historical analysis and insights will be lost

Alternative: Revoke Twitter/X Access Only

If you only want to disconnect your Twitter/X account without deleting your Ibtikar account data:

  1. Go to your Twitter/X account on the web or mobile app
  2. Navigate to Settings and privacy → Security and account access → Apps and sessions → Connected apps
  3. Find "Ibtikar" in the list of connected apps
  4. Click "Revoke access" or "Disconnect"
  5. Confirm the disconnection

Note: Revoking Twitter/X access will prevent the app from functioning, but your Ibtikar account data will remain stored. To fully delete your account, follow the deletion process above.

Data Deletion Under GDPR and CCPA

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data under GDPR (Article 17) or CCPA. We will honor these requests and delete your data as described above.

To exercise your right to deletion, please contact us at ibtikarpal@gmail.com with:

  • Subject line: "GDPR/CCPA Deletion Request" or "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your Twitter/X username
  • Confirmation that you want to delete your account

We will process your request within 30 days as required by law.

Before You Delete

Consider the following before deleting your account:

  • Make sure you have exported or saved any data you want to keep (if export features are available)
  • Understand that deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • If you're having issues with the app, consider contacting support first - we may be able to help
  • You can always revoke Twitter/X access first to stop data collection without deleting your account
